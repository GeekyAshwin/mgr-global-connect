✦ Our Story

Passionate About Curated Travel

MGR Global Connect was born from a simple belief — travel should be transformative, personal, and accessible. Founded by Rahul Singh Rana, we started as a small travel enthusiast group in Dehradun and have grown into a trusted travel company serving hundreds of explorers across India.

Our Mission

To create meaningful travel experiences that bring people together and foster cultural understanding.

Small Group Focus

We cap groups at 10-12 people ensuring personalized attention and genuine connections on every trip.

Led by Rahul Singh Rana

CEO & lead planner with a passion for exploring cultures and building lasting memories for travelers.
